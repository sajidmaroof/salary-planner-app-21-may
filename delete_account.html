Delete Your Account & Data

App: Salary Planner  |  Developer: ZoneStudio

This page explains how to request deletion of your Salary Planner account and all associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account and all your data, send an email to:

zonestudio786@gmail.com

Please include:

  • The email address you used to sign in to the app
  • The subject line: "Delete My Account"

We will process your request and permanently delete your account within 7 business days.

What Data Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, we permanently delete:

  • Your account information (name, email)
  • All your salary and budget settings
  • All your expense records
  • All your monthly reports and history

Data Retention

All data is deleted permanently and cannot be recovered. We do not retain any personal data after account deletion is complete.
